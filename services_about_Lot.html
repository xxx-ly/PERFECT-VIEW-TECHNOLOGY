Hardware Platforms for IoT

From manufacturing and healthcare to retail and beyond, we help organisations build robust IoT ecosystems powered by diverse sensors—including infrared, proximity, thermal, pressure, and more. These devices communicate through a range of protocols such as Wi-Fi, cellular, Zigbee, NFC, and RFID, and are supported by gateways, routers, and mobile devices—all seamlessly integrated within a secure cloud environment

IoT Software Development

No matter the scale, an IoT network requires reliable software to manage and analyse the data collected from sensors and connected devices. This software can take the form of a streamlined web dashboard, an intuitive mobile app, or a fully customised solution tailored to your business needs. At PERFECT VIEW TECHNOLOGY, our IoT Lab provides the expertise to design and build secure, scalable, and efficient software foundations—empowering your organisation to unlock the full potential of its IoT ecosystem

Building a Prototype

At PERFECT VIEW TECHNOLOGY AI & IoT Lab, we help clients transform ideas into tangible prototypes to validate how their IoT solutions work in real-world environments. Before creating a hardware prototype, the right firmware is essential. Our team can develop firmware for platforms such as NodeMCU, Contiki, RIOT OS, FreeRTOS, TinyOS, or Arm Mbed OS. When it comes to hardware prototyping, we primarily use development boards and tools like Arduino®, Particle Photon™, Raspberry Pi®, and BeagleBone®. At the same time, our engineers are fully capable of working with the boards and components you already have available, ensuring flexibility and seamless integration.

Manufacturing

If you need mechanical prototyping and manufacturing, we would turn to our partners in China. Our IoT lab uses prototyping tools that include 3D Printing, Molding, and CNC machinery.
